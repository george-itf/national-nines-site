ESSEX N|NES

Pairs Matchplay Knockout

Finals: October 3-4, 2026 at London Beach Golf Club

How It Works

1

Enter Your Pair

Register your pair from any 9-hole club in Essex. Open to all handicap levels.

2

Knockout Rounds

Pairs are drawn against each other. Play your match at the away team's course. Winner progresses.

3

Semi Finals & Final

Last 4 pairs travel to London Beach for an epic finals weekend with overnight stay.

Competition Format

Matchplay Rules

  • • Fourball Better Ball matchplay
  • • Full handicap difference applies
  • • Strokes given on stroke index
  • • All matches over 9 holes
  • • Sudden death playoff if tied

Match Arrangements

  • • Away team travels to opponent's course
  • • Agree date within round deadline
  • • Green fees paid by away pair
  • • Report results to National Nines
  • • 2 weeks per round
