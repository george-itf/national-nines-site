About National Nines

Bringing competitive tournament golf to 9-hole clubs across Great Britain & Ireland.

Our Mission

National Nines Golf was founded with a simple belief: great competitive golf shouldn't be limited to 18-hole courses. Across the UK and Ireland, thousands of passionate golfers are members of excellent 9-hole clubs, yet often miss out on the tournament experiences enjoyed by larger clubs.

We're here to change that. Our competitions bring together golfers from 9-hole clubs for properly organised, exciting tournament golf with real prizes and unforgettable experiences.

Community

Building connections between 9-hole clubs and their members, creating a community of like-minded golfers who love the game.

Competition

Professional, well-organised tournaments that give every golfer the chance to test themselves in competitive play.

Experience

Memorable tournament experiences including overnight stays, dinners, and the chance to play fantastic courses.

Giving Back

All proceeds from our shop support grassroots golf and help us expand National Nines to more regions.
