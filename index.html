Championing 9-Hole Golf

National Nines Golf was created to give members of 9-hole golf clubs the opportunity to compete in properly organised tournament golf, just like the bigger clubs enjoy.

We believe great golf doesn't need 18 holes. Our competitions bring together golfers from 9-hole clubs across the country for exciting, competitive events with fantastic prizes and memorable experiences.
